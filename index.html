5 Simple Steps for Saving the World
1
Watch out for meteors! They come in 5 types (marked by color and symbol), and it only takes a few hits to destroy the planet.
2
Connect matching pairs of satellites to set up defenses against meteors of the same type. Connections can't cross Earth.
3
Use your joystick to fly through space. Grab satellites by pressing and holding either joystick button.
4
When both players are holding satellites, press the center button to connect them!
5
Connections are one-time-use. They break when meteors break. So be sure to keep making new ones!
